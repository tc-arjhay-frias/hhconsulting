What we do?

Contractual Claims

We assist our clients in submitting or defending claims by offering claims advisory support, reviewing submitted claims (including design, time, or commercial claims), assessing documentation, evaluating communication procedures, and providing guidance for fair assessment, impact analysis on the project schedule, and future claim considerations.

Dispute Resolution

Mediation offers a private, structured negotiation with a neutral third party, ensuring binding agreements and allowing the management team to maintain control and preserve future relationships. In contrast, arbitration is a formal, private, and binding process where a designated third-party arbitrator reviews arguments and evidence, ultimately determining the rights and obligations of each party involved.
