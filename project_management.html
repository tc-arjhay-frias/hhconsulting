PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Going beyond the traditional approach to construction and procurement, we use experience and expertise to carefully guide our clients through the process of design, tendering, construction and close out.

WHAT DO WE DO?

Project & Programme Management

We provide comprehensive Project & Programme Management, guiding projects from start to finish, ensuring precision in planning, resource management, safety compliance, and constant client updates during all construction phases.

Project Troubleshooting & Rescue

Initially acting as a circuit break, we cut across all aspects of the project to understand the issues. Having pinpointed failures we quickly develop a robust Project Recovery Programme and install new project controls.

Design Management

The success of a construction project relies greatly on the quality of the design it is based upon. Our team drives, strategizes and streamlines the design team’s creative process to ensure the delivery of an integrated and comprehensive design that meets our Clients' project vision and goals.

Operations & Maintenance

Prior to handing over the property, we make sure to schedule a training session between your household and suppliers on how critical equipment should be operated and maintained to guarantee proper care. A full manual in hard and soft copy are provided as reference. Where clients prefer that we oversee this instead, we offer a monthly inspection and reporting services that includes overseeing any repairs and warranties.
