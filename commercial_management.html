WHAT DO WE DO?

Cost & Commercial Management

With a strong reputation for safeguarding our Clients' finances via rigorous budgeting and monitoring, we have facilitated the successful execution of the largest development projects in the Philippines, recognizing that cashflow, akin to a project's lifeblood, necessitates meticulous management and equilibrium during the construction program, offering pre-contract services such as estimating and cost planning, tender and procurement, and post-contract services like financial statements/cost control, contract administration, contractor's progress claims/progress payments, change orders/variations, and final accounts.

Insurance Valuations

The Philippines is in a natural disaster prone location. Our vital annual service continues to help companies and their respective Boards understand the state of their real estate portfolios throughout the whole life cycle, identifying key risks that may lead to the repair or rebuilding of those structures. Accurate scenario planning coupled with up to date construction cost data, allows Financial Institutions, Insurance Providers, Investors and Developers to determine if they are under insured, make the necessary changes to their premiums and avoid abrogation.

Construction Loan Monitoring

We help Financial Institutions and Private Investors understand the potential exposure of their loans on commercial real estate or land development. Strict monitoring of claimed or stated progress or purchase of materials, against that on site allows the early detection of potential risks to a loan.

Strategic Development Consulting

Turning our Clients' vision into a cohesive strategic brief, detailing the key elements to delivering a successful project. Maximising the value achieved from the capital investment through embedding best practices through a clear Project Execution Plan.

Bid Consultancy

Strategic advice on how to improve your chances of successfully biding on a project. Drive value in your proposal while reducing the amount of risk taken on. Having a clear bid strategy is essential to improving your chances of winning.
