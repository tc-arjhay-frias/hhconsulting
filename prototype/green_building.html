What we do?

LEED Certification

LEED is the most widely used score-based rating system in the world. it focuses on providing a perspective framework for high performing new and existing buildings. LEED certified office buildings, on average consume 23% less electricity and 24% less water when compared to conventional office buildings which translates to an annual operating-cost reduction.

WELL Certification

WELL is the world's first evidence based rating system designed for measuring, certifying and monitoring building features that impact the building occupant's health and wellbeing. It's recent iteration - WELL version 2, launched last September 2020 signifies the rapid growth of the tool and a recognition of its utility in creating healthy indoor spaces especially during the COVID-19 pandemic.

BERDE Certification

BERDE (Building for Ecologically Responsible Design Excellence) is the Philippines' national voluntary green building rating system. Several local governments like Mandaluyong and Cebu city are adopting it as their basis to mandate green building certification in their localities. Furthermore, legislation in cities like Mandaue and Pasig give private building owners tax incentives for achieving high BERDE ratings.
